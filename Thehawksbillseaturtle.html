Something About Hawksbill Sea Turtle

The Hawkbill sea turtle is seen to have a flat body shape. Some other features it includes is a hard carapace and has flipper like limbs that have adapted for the swiming, as it is found in the open ocean. Some qualities that make them different then other sea turtles is the sharp curving beak it has. Something I find quite interesting about them is that their shells can change colors depending on the water temperature. They can also be found in lagoons or coral reefs.
